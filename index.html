Tesla Turbine

The Tesla turbine is a bladeless turbine that uses viscosity and the boundary layer effect to extract energy from a fluid. Fluid is injected tangentially onto smooth, parallel discs, causing them to rotate through viscous drag. This unique design offers simplicity and versatility, finding applications in various fields like micro-power generation.
